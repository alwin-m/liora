# Liora Design Philosophy (v2.5) — The Companion Era

Liora is evolving from a data-tracking tool into a **meaningful companion**. Taking inspiration from applications like **Forest** (peaceful/organic) and **Duolingo** (playful/gamified), we are rebuilding the experience to be psychologically safe, visually delightful, and emotionally supportive for girls and women.

## 🎨 Color Palette & Psychology

We are moving away from dark, clinical, or overly bold corporate colors. Instead, we use a palette that evokes nature, growth, and softness.

| Color | Hex | Psychology | Role |
|-------|-----|------------|------|
| **Liora Sage** | `#98B4A6` | Organic, calm, growth, safety. | Primary Action, Healing, Forest Vibe |
| **Blush Petal** | `#FADADD` | Softness, care, friendliness. | Secondary, Highlights, Comfort |
| **Warm Sand** | `#FFFDF0` | Warmth, readability, approachability. | Page Background, Cards |
| **Deep Moss** | `#4A5D4E` | Grounding, serious but natural. | Text, Icons, High Contrast |
| **Sun Glow** | `#FFD27D` | Optimism, energy, warmth. | Highlights, Fertile Window |

## 🌟 Visual Principles

1. **Organic Shapes**: Replace sharp corners with extremely rounded "blob" or "stone" shapes (Inspired by Forest's trees and Duolingo's characters).
2. **Meaningful Graphics**: Use illustrations that feel hand-drawn or high-quality vector art rather than generic system icons.
3. **Gamified Mindfulness**: Logging data shouldn't feel like "entering data"—it should feel like "checking in with a friend" or "nourishing a garden".
4. **Fluid Transitions**: Every action should feel bouncy and alive. Buttons should have a subtle "squish" when pressed.

## 🛠️ Implementation Plan

### 1. Style Definition
- Update `ThemeData` with the new Sage/Blush palette.
- Use `GoogleFonts.Outfit` for a modern, friendly typography.

### 2. Home Screen Overhaul
- The "System Forecast" card should feel like a message from a friend, using Sage and Sand tones.
- The "Blood Cube" will be styled as a **"Nectar Reservoir"** with flowing liquid, making it feel less medical and more mystical/enjoyable.

### 3. Interactive Tracking
- The AirPods-style popup will use **Sage** for primary buttons and **Sun Glow** for emphasis.
- Ensure the progress slider feels like a "growth meter".

---
> [!TIP]
> This design language reduces health anxiety by presenting biological cycles as a natural, rhythmic part of life, rather than a clinical condition to be managed.
